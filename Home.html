Talk shows on hot topics

We organise talk shows twice a week to showcase attention-worthy current topics in society. Our guest speakers include Mr Pranav Tumminkatti, a veteran in social advocacy and a professor at Yale University. You can catch our talkshows live at FM 92.4, Channel 5 from 8 to 9 pm every Monday and Friday, or on YouTube where we post all our talkshows.

Forums discussing existential issues

Our forums are a safe space for you to express your views and opinions on current hot topics and issues. Join fellow citizens to debate on a particular issue. All the proposed solutions will be looked through and if deemed well thought of, some may even be proposed to the government and brought up in parliment. Associated contributors will also receive valuable credits. So what are you waiting for? Start posting now!

Raising funds to tackle these issues

Our company has been allocated SGD 3.2 million by the government to fund psoposals to tackle issues in society. We will carefully use these funds to financially support anybody who can come up with a reasonable solution to a problem in society. You will also be paired up with an expert in that certain field and will work to implement this solution and tackle the problem. Don't lose the valuable and life-changing opportunity! Start thinking and start posting today!
